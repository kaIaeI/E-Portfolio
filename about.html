About Me

My name is Kalael Gileon Magnus Ortiz Dabao, and I am a student in the Computer Systems Technology (CST) program at Saskatchewan Polytechnic. I am building foundational skills in computer hardware, networking, programming, and web development while exploring different areas of IT.

I chose CST because I’ve always been interested in understanding how technology works—from computers and networks to software and systems. I enjoy hands-on problem solving, learning new tools, and building projects that help me grow my technical skills.

My goals are to continue strengthening my programming abilities, improve my troubleshooting skills, and develop a strong understanding of system administration. This portfolio highlights the projects I’ve completed and the progress I’m making as I work toward a future career in the IT field.
